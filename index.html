Online Event Reservation System for Regina's Garden and Restaurant

Capstone Project, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, STI College Dagupan

Problem/Goal:

  • Problem: Regina's Garden and Restaurant traditionally relies on manual, pen-and-paper methods for managing client reservation requests. This leads to inefficiencies such as staff manually searching through files, time-consuming processes, and individual texting/messaging of clients for reservation updates. These manual approaches are inefficient, do not scale well for large numbers of clients or data, and can lead to redundant tasks and slower business operations. Scheduling conflicts were also a primary challenge in existing manual reservation systems referenced.
  • Goal: The project aims to integrate technology into Regina's Garden and Restaurant by developing a web-based event reservation system. This system is intended to streamline day-to-day operations, enhance the reservation process, improve client data management, increase productivity, and facilitate better staff-client communication. It seeks to provide a user-friendly digital platform for event management, scheduling, and organization, ultimately improving efficiency, client satisfaction, and competitive positioning.

Your Role:

  • As the leader of the group, I was primarily responsible for all coding aspects of the Online Event Reservation System. My key contributions included:
    • Leading the project team and overseeing the development process.
    • Coding the entire system, encompassing both front-end and back-end development.
    • Integrating the ideas, designs, and client requirements gathered by the other two group members into the functional system.
    • Coordinating with stakeholders to ensure the system met the specific needs of Regina's Garden and Restaurant.
  • The other two members of the group contributed significantly by sharing ideas, assisting with design aspects, and gathering critical information regarding the client's needs and wants for the system.

Technologies Used:

  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, AJAX.
  • Back-end: PHP (potentially Node.js or Java).
  • Database: MySQL.
  • Server/Development Environment: Apache, XAMPP.
  • Email Sending: PHPMailer.
  • Security: SSL encryption.
  • Tools: Notepad++, Visual Studio Code (VS Code), phpMyAdmin.

Key Features/Functionality:

  • Online Event Booking: Customers can book events online.
  • Venue and Menu Viewing: Clients can view food packages, catering and event services, venue details, pictures of food, utensils, and the venue's exterior and interior.
  • Centralized Data Management: A centralized database for staff to streamline the management of reservations, client data, and event schedules.
  • Real-time Reservation Updates: The system provides real-time updates on reservation status.
  • Automated Notifications: SMS notifications and email instructions for payment. Email reminders for upcoming reservations and pending verification requests.
  • Client Information Access: Staff can view and access clients' data and booking reservations from the website.
  • Flexible Payment Options: Support for various payment methods, including cash remittance and bank deposit instructions.
  • Feedback and Reviews: Allows users to provide feedback and reviews about their reservation experience.
  • Comprehensive Event Information Display: Displays a list of events with details such as name, date, time, location, description, and images. Provides comprehensive information about each event, including agenda, speakers, performers, and venue details.
  • Admin Portal Functions: Staff personnel on the admin portal can verify confirmation and cancellation of reservation requests upon payment receipt.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for PC, laptop, and mobile devices.

Outcome/Impact:

  • Operational Efficiency: Improves efficiency by eliminating manual methods, reducing scheduling conflicts, and streamlining booking processes.
  • Client Satisfaction: Enhances customer satisfaction through improved communication, easy online booking, and clearer information.
  • Competitive Positioning: Transforms Regina's Garden and Restaurant's operations, offering a scalable and adaptable solution that provides a competitive edge.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Offers valuable insights by tracking metrics like scheduled reservations, cancellations, and customer satisfaction, aiding in service enhancement.
  • Improved Communication: Facilitates smooth communication between organizers and attendees.
  • Enhanced Technical Abilities: Staff members can improve their technical abilities by learning to use and maintain the system.
  • Practical Experience for Developers: Provides developers with hands-on experience in designing, developing, and implementing a real-world system, enhancing their technical skills and employability.
